Publishing

Charleston Magazine is a well-regarded publication in South Carolina that covers the lifestyle, culture, food, and events of Charleston, with a wide readership that includes locals and visitors. The editorial team is dedicated to producing content that highlights the city's vibrant character and diverse offerings.

As an Editorial Intern, I was responsible for writing and editing various pieces for the publication. My writing involved researching and crafting compelling content about the city’s wedding scene, its rich history, and its vibrant arts community. In my editorial role, I ensured that all content was accurate, consistent, and aligned with the magazine’s voice and style. Because of my eye for detail, I was also asked to edit key pieces such as the "In the Neighborhood" section.

Communication with artists’ publicists, local park representatives, and museum staff was key to ensuring accurate event coverage. I worked closely with the editorial team to make each piece clear, engaging, and polished.
