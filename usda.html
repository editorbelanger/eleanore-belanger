Scientific and Technical Editing

The Economic Research Service (ERS) is an agency within the U.S. Department of Agriculture that publishes data-driven research on agriculture and economics. The ERS publishing team ensures that all research is accurate, consistent, unbiased, and ready for a global audience.

As a Technical Writer/Editor, I contributed to a variety of publications, including research reports, Outlook reports, visual insights, and data tables.

My editor title came into play as I ensured the content was grammatically correct, factually accurate, and stylistically consistent. My writer title came into play as I translated complex research findings into clear, compelling summaries tailored for nontechnical readers such as policymakers.

From quick-turnaround Outlooks to in-depth research reports, I ensure scientific and technical publications meet rigorous editorial standards.
